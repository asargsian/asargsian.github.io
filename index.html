About me

Anahit is an instructor supporting the Social Research and Public Policy program at the Social Science division at NYU Abu Dhabi. She received her Master of Engineering from American University of Armenia. After graduation, she joined the Computer Science Department at Masdar Institute as a visiting scholar. Her work lies in the intersection of Computational Social Science, Data Science and AI. Her most recent projects include the analysis of the interpretability and identification of targeted indicators of academic success, and the study of football teamsâ€™ evolution, using machine learning and computational techniques.
